Selected research and projects

SIGHT

SIGHT: From Imagination to Evidence via Active Spatial Hypothesis Testing

First Author · AAAI 2027

We introduce SIGHT (Spatial Inference through Generative Hypothesis Testing), a training-free test-time framework that treats world-model-assisted spatial reasoning as active hypothesis testing. SIGHT converts answer choices into falsifiable hypotheses and selects camera probes predicted to distinguish them.
